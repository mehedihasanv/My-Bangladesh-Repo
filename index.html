Dr. Muhammad Yunus believes that poverty is not created by the poor but by societal structures and policies. He emphasizes that the poor have the ability to lift themselves out of poverty if given the right opportunities, stating, The poor themselves can create a poverty-free world. All we have to do is to free them from the chains that we have put around them. He challenges the traditional approach of charity, arguing that it removes initiative and responsibility from individuals, saying, Charity is not the answer. Charity only perpetuates poverty by taking away initiative and responsibility from people. Yunus strongly advocates for social business and microfinance, explaining, A charity dollar has only one life; a social business dollar can be invested over and over again.
